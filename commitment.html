How we view Tea 4 Two

A safe place to go to

Tea 4 Two was born on the idea that it would be a place where one another would help each other...

It is a place were you can share your passions with other customers and in the meantime learn from them!

A way to help one another

At Tea 4 Two we have a goal that is to help people seeking for a job and face difficulties to find one.

The idea is to pair a person that is in a profesionnal reintegration phase and pair him/her with a young person looking for a job. That way the young person can learn from the experience acquiered by the other person and vice versa.

A place where local and small producers thrive

Local produces are important for us, it helps the local community to thrive and gives us quality products which will have a low carbon footprint due to the fact that they are not travelling across the globe to get to our tea room.

That way we ensure the quality of our products and promote the local producers around us!
